Item 5. Other Events.

The Registrant issued the following press release on February 7, 1994:

ALCOA REDUCES PRIMARY ALUMINUM PRODUCTION

Pittsburgh, Pa., February 7, 1994 -- Alcoa will reduce primary aluminum 
production in it U.S. operations by 100,000 metric tons a year. The 
reductions will be made at Rockdale, Texas and Wenatchee, Washington.
Implementing actions will be initiated immediately.
