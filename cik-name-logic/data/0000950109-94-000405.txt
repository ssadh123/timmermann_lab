Item 5. Other Events.

Effective on February 23, 1994, Mark S. Manafo ceased to be a Director, Chief
Operating Officer and employee of Partech Communications Group, Inc. ("PCG"), a
wholly-owned subsidiary of Partech Holdings Corporation ("Partech"), and all
PCG's subsidiaries. Also on that date, Mr. Manafo's options to purchase 160,000
shares of Partech's stock terminated and his options to purchase 90,00 shares
will terminate three (3) months thereafter.
