Item 5. Other Events.

On October 26, 1994, Americold Corporation (the "Company") settled,
subject to court approval, the claim submitted by the United States
Attorney for the District of Kansas on behalf of the United States
Department of Agriculture in connection with the December 1991 fire
at the Company's Kansas City, Kansas warehouse facility. The
amount of the settlement, together with the amount of prior
settlements, did not exceed the aggregate amount of the Company's
applicable insurance coverage and will require no cash payment by
the Company. The Company has now settled all of the lawsuits and
claims related to the Kansas City, Kansas fire.
