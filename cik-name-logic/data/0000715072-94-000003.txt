ITEM 2: ACQUISITION OR DISPOSITION OF ASSETS
------------------------------------

On April 15, 1994, The Peoples Bank and Trust Company,
a wholly-owned subsidiary of The Peoples Holding Company
assumed the deposits and purchased selected assets of 
three offices of Security Federal Savings and Loan 
Association. These offices are located in Hernando,
New Albany, and Southaven and officially opened as 
The Peoples Bank at 9:00 a.m. Monday, April 18.

Upon completion of the acquisition, the bank's assets
increased from approximately $760 million to $790 
million. The addition of the three offices of Security
brings the bank's total number of offices to forty-two,
operating in twenty-seven communities and seventeen 
counties in north and north central Mississippi.
