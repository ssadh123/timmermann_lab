Item 5. Other Events

On January 28, 1994 the Company was notified by Nasdaq that an
exception to the bid price and market value of the public float
requirements of the Nasdaq National Market would not be granted. 
Accordingly the securities of American Nuclear Corporation will be
moved to the Nasdaq SmallCap Market effective February 1, 1994.
