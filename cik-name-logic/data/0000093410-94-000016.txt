Item 5. Other Events.
------------

On August 11, 1994, Chevron filed a Form 8-K which contained, as
Exhibit 99.1, the August 1, 1994 First Supplemental Indenture to
the May 15, 1987 original Indenture between Chevron Capital U.S.A.
Inc., Chevron Corporation, and The Chase Manhattan Bank (National
Association). Chevron is filing as part of this Amendment No. 1 on
Form 8-K/A a revised page 28 to Exhibit 99.1 which corrects the
list of authorized officers eligible to sign on behalf of Chevron
Capital U.S.A., Inc. and Chevron Corporation.
