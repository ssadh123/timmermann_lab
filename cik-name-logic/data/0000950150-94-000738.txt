ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS.

On November 17, 1994, Hilton Hotels Corporation announced that it has
engaged the investment banking firm of Smith Barney to undertake a study and
make recommendations to management and the Board of Directors for strategic
alternatives to enhance shareholder value. The company said these alternatives
may include a sale of the entire company in one or a series of transactions;
spin-off of one or more of its businesses; recapitalization; business
combination; share repurchase program; or similar transaction.
