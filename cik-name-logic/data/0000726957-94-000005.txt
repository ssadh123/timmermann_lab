Item 5. Other Events.

On March 25, 1994, at a Special Meeting of the Registrant's Board of
Directors, the Board voted to seek the sale of the Registrant. This action 
followed the announcement by Dorothy D. Park, in her capacity as
Personal Representative of the Estate of Roy H. Park (the "Estate"), of
the Estate's decision to sell the Estate-held shares of the Registrant,
current amounting to approximately 89.65% of the Registrant's outstanding 
common stock.
