Item 5. Other Events

On March 22, 1994, Riggs National Corporation (the "Corporation") 
completed the redemption of $120.7 million principal amount of subordinated 
notes due 1996. The notes were redeemed with all of the net proceeds from the
sale in January of $125 million principal amount of 8.5% subordinated notes 
due February 1, 2006.

The Corporation redeemed the entire $51.5 million outstanding of its 
Floating Rate Subordinated Notes due 1996, and $69.2 million out of the $95.3 
million outstanding of its Floating Rate Subordinated Capital Notes due 1996.

Pursuant to the requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, 
the registrant has duly caused this report to be signed on its behalf by the 
undersigned, thereunto duly authorized.
