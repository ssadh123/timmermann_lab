Item 8. Change in Fiscal Year.

The board of directors of Westwood One, Inc. (the "Company") approved, 
effective August 31, 1994, the change of the Company's fiscal year from a 
November 30 year end, the fiscal year end used in its most recent filing with 
the Securities and Exchange Commission, to the new fiscal year end of 
December 31. The report on Form 10 - Q for the fiscal quarter ended 
September 30, 1994 will be the form on which the report covering the 
transition period will be filed by the Company.
