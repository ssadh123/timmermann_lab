ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS

On June 7, 1994, Allegheny Ludlum Corporation announced that the
Company and the United Steelworkers of America (USWA) had reached a
new multi-year labor agreement ending a ten week strike which began on
April 1 and affected most of the Company's plants. Under the
agreement with the USWA, which covers 3,500 of the Company's 6,000
employees, employees began to return to work on June 9, 1994. A mail
ballot ratification vote is expected to be completed by the end of
June.
