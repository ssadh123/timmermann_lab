Item 5. Other Events

New England Power Company (NEP), a subsidiary of New England
Electric System, announced on December 8, 1994, that it will not
increase its base wholesale electricity rates until at least 1997
if its wholesale rate agreement, filed on December 7, 1994 with
the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC), is approved. 
The agreement avoids any increase in NEP's base rates associated
with the completion and placing into service of the re-powered
Manchester Street Station in Providence, R.I. NEP has not
increased its base rates since 1991. The agreement is subject to
FERC approval.
