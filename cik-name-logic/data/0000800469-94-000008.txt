Item 5. Other Events

On November 16, 1994, Resorts International Hotel, Inc.
("RIH"), purchased 12,899 Units comprising $12,899,000 principal
amount of Junior Mortgage Notes due 2004 issued by Resorts
International Hotel Financing, Inc. ("RIHF") and 12,899 shares of
Class B Redeemable Common Stock issued by Resorts International,
Inc. ("RII") at a price of $6,740,000. RIH and RIHF are both
wholly-owned subsidiaries of RII. RIH, the owner and operator of
Merv Griffin's Resorts Casino Hotel in Atlantic City, New Jersey,
is the source of debt service and the guarantor of the Junior
Mortgage Notes issued by RIHF. A total of 35,000 Units were issued
as part of the reorganization of RII in May 1994.
