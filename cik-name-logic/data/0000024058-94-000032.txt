Item 5. Other Events.

On July 18, 1994, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System
approved the proposed merger of Continental Bank Corporation ("Continental")
with and into BankAmerica Corporation ("BAC"), subject to compliance with
certain conditions. Continental expects the merger to close within a few
weeks after the expiration of the mandatory 30-day waiting period under the
Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act of 1976, subject to satisfaction
of the remaining conditions set forth in the merger agreement between BAC and
Continental.
