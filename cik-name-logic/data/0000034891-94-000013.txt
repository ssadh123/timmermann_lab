INFORMATION INCLUDED IN THIS REPORT

ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS

Federal Paper Board Company, Inc. (the "Company") hereby reports
that it has reached an agreement with Bankers Trust Company with
respect to various financial instruments. Under the terms of
the agreement, two foreign currency forward contracts and caps,
which the Company recorded at mark to market in the first,
second and third quarters, were canceled resulting in a fourth
quarter $12 million pre-tax gain. The Company remains a party
to two other financial arrangements with Bankers Trust, one of
which will be marked to market on a quarterly basis and the
other is accounted for on a hedge accounting basis. Both of
these arrangements terminate in July, 1998. The Company has
agreed that except for fulfilling the terms of these financial
arrangements, Bankers Trust will have no further
responsibilities to the Company in regard to any such
arrangements.
