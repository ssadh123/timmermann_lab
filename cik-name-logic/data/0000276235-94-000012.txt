September 14, 1994

CENTRAL FIDELITY APPOINTS LEWIS N. MILLER, JR.
CHAIRMAN OF THE BOARD
AND CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

Richmond, VA -- At its regularly scheduled meeting today, The Board of 
Directors of Central Fidelity Banks, Inc. (NASDAQ-CFBS) appointed 
Lewis N. Miller, Jr. Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer. 
Miller, 50, who was President and Co-Chief Executive Officer, has 
been with the Company since 1972.

Miller joined Central Fidelity predecessor, the Central National 
Corporation, as Planning Manager. He was named President in 1984. 
In 1993, Miller was named President and Co-Chief Executive Officer.

Miller succeeds Carroll L. Saine, who passed away in August.

Central Fidelity Banks, Inc. is a Richmond, Virginia-based bank 
holding company with $9.6 billion in assets and 230 offices.
