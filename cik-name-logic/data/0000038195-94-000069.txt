ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS

The Company has received a Civil Investigative Demand (CID) issued by 
the U.S. Department of Justice, Antitrust Division pursuant to the Antitrust 
Civil Process Act, Title 15 of the United States Code. The CID seeks 
documents and information as part of an Antitrust Division civil investigation 
to determine whether there are agreements in restraint of trade in connection 
with sales of sanitary paper products. The Company is cooperating with the 
investigation.
