Item 8. Change in Fiscal Year.

On May 25, 1994, the Board of Directors of the Registrant amended
By-Law No. 48 changing the Registrant's fiscal year end from June 30 of each
year to that Friday in each year closest to June 30. The change in
Registrant's fiscal year shall commence with the 1994 fiscal year and 
the transition period resulting from such change will be first reflected in
Registrant's Annual Report on Form 10-K for that fiscal year.
