Item 5. Other Events

On February 23, 1994, the State of New York Banking
Department ("NYSBD") advised North Fork Bank (the "Bank"), the
primary subsidiary of North Fork Bancorporation, Inc. (the
"Company"), based on the findings of their October 1, 1993
examination, which was conducted jointly with examiners from the
Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, that in consideration of the
improvement in the Bank's financial condition, the Memorandum of
Understanding ("MOU") dated August 25, 1993 was terminated. The
terms of the MOU were disclosed in the Company's Form 8-K filed on
September 9, 1993.
The Company received similar notification from the Federal
Deposit Insurance Corporation on February 17, 1994. Receipt of that
notification was described in the Company's Form 8K filed February
22, 1994.<PAGE>
