STANLEY DECLARES FIRST QUARTER DIVIDEND

New Britain, Connecticut...The Board of Directors of The Stanley

Works today declared a regular first quarter dividend of $.34 per

share on the common stock. The dividend is payable March 31,

1994 to shareholders of record at the close of business on March

14, 1994. Dividend payments in 1994 mark the 118th consecutive

year in which the company has paid dividends, a record for all

industrials listed on the New York Stock Exchange. 

The Stanley Works is a worldwide producer of tools, hardware and

specialty hardware for consumer, home improvement, industrial and

professional use.
