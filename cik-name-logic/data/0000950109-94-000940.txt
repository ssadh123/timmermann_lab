Item 5. Other Events
------------

On June 2, 1994, the Registrant's parent company, UAL Corporation
(the "Company"), entered into an amendment to the Agreement and Plan of
Recapitalization, dated as of March 25, 1994 (the "Amendment to the
Recapitalization Agreement"), with the Air Line Pilots Association,
International UAL-MEC and the International Association of Machinists and
Aerospace Workers, amending the Recapitalization Agreement initially executed
on March 25, 1994 (the "Initial Recapitalization Agreement") and certain
Schedules and Exhibits thereto. Capitalized terms used but not defined herein
shall have the meaning ascribed to such terms in the Amended and Restated
Agreement and Plan of Recapitalization, dated as of March 25, 1994, a copy of
which is attached hereto as Exhibit A to the Amendment to the Recapitalization
Agreement and incorporated herein by reference. The Initial Recapitalization
Agreement was filed as part of the Company's Current Report on Form 8-K dated
March 28, 1994. A copy of the Amendment to the Recapitalization Agreement and
all the Exhibits thereto were attached as Exhibit 10.1 to the Company's
Current Report on Form 8-K dated June 3, 1994 and are incorporated herein as
an Exhibit.
