SEABOARD CORPORATION

Item 5. Other Events.

The Delaware Chancery Court has approved the settlement of a
stockholders' derivative action brought in April 1990 against the
Company and certain subsidiaries, its parent Seaboard Flour
Corporation (SFC) and the directors of the Company at that time.
A Summary of Proposed Settlement was mailed by the Company to its
stockholders on or about August 31, 1994. The Settlement was
approved on substantially the terms set forth in the Summary.
Under the settlement, the Company will receive $10,800,000 from
SFC and the directors. Delaware Chancery Court has also approved
payment from the settlement proceeds of plaintiff's legal fees in
the amount of $2,000,000. The net proceeds will be recorded as
an increase to Additional Capital.
