Item 5. Other Events

On January 27, 1994, the Trust sold its Marin General Hospital
property in Larkspur, California. The sales price was $12,412,351,
resulting in a gain of $3,625,351. The Trust used the net proceeds of this
sale to reduce the outstanding balance on its line of credit. In 1993, the
Trust received $1,324,852 in rental income from this property.
