ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS.

A meeting of the Board of Directors of Registrant was held on October 18,
1994, immediately following Registrant's Annual Shareholders' Meeting. During
the meeting of the Board of Directors of Registrant, the Board approved the
repurchase, from time to time, of the Registrant's common stock for general
corporate purposes. Upon conclusion of the meeting of the Board of Directors,
Registrant included the following disclosure in a press release issued and dated
October 18, 1994:

"The Company also, announced that its Board approved the repurchase, from
time to time, of the Company's common stock for general corporate purposes.

" `The Company purchased shares in the past for general corporate purposes'
stated Robert D. Rogers, President. `This authorization reaffirms the
Board's desire to again purchase shares when prices are attractive.' "
