Item 5. Other Events
------------

On July 12, 1994, St. Paul Bancorp, Inc. (the "Company") announced a second
program to repurchase up to 964,422 shares (approximately 5%) of its currently
outstanding common stock over the next six months in the open market (and
privately negotiated transactions). Under its previous repurchase program, the
Company purchased 275,950 shares of its common stock.
