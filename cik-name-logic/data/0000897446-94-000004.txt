Item 5. Other Events

On December 17, 1993, Empire State Broadcasting
Corporation, an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of Price
Communication Corporation, agreed to sell to Keymarket of
Buffalo, Inc., substantially all the assets, together with
certain liabilities, relating to the operation of radio broadcast
stations WWKB(AM) and WKSE(FM), licensed respectively to Buffalo,
New York, and Rochester, New York, for $5,000,000. The closing
is expected to occur during the first half of 1994, subject to
approval of the Federal Communications Commission.
