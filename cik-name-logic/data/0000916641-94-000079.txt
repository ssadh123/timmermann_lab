Item 4(b) Changes in Registrant's Certifying Accountant

On August 18, 1994, the Registrant engaged the firm of Arthur
Andersen & Co. as the Registrant's independent accountant. The
change in accountants was recommended by the Audit Committee of the
Registrant and approved by the Board of Directors.

Prior to that date, the firm of Coopers & Lybrand L.L.P. had been
the Registrant's independent accountant.
