ITEM 5. On December 8, 1994, Providian Corporation
("Providian") announced that Shailesh Mehta had been elected
President and Chief Operating Officer of Providian by
Providian's Board of Directors. In addition, he was elected
a Director of Providian. He has been Executive Vice
President of Providian since August 1993. In this capacity,
he has been serving as chief executive officer of two of
Providian's operating businesses, Providian Bancorp and
Providian Direct Insurance. In his new position, he will
continue to oversee these two businesses, as well as
Providian's Agency Group business. Lee Adrean will remain
President of Agency Group.
