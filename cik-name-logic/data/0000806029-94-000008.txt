PRESS RELEASE DATED: November 21, 1994

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Equitable Real Estate Shopping Centers L.P. (NYSE-"EQM") announced today that
it is changing its name to Midwest Real Estate Shopping Center L.P., effective
immediately. Units of Midwest Real Estate Shopping Center L.P. will continue
to trade on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol "EQM". 

The name change is in conjunction with the termination of the former asset
management agreement with Equitable Real Estate Investment Management, Inc.,
pursuant to which the Partnership agreed to cease using the words "Equitable"
and "Equitable Real Estate" in its name. The new name also better reflects the
present assets of the Partnership, which currently constitute one regional
shopping mall in Brooklyn Center, Minnesota. The partnership had formerly
owned another regional shopping mall in Detroit, Michigan which was sold in
July 1994.
