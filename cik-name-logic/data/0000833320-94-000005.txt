BURLINGTON RESOURCES INC.

NAMES NEW CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

AT MERIDIAN OIL INC.

Houston, Texas, October 10, 1994 -

Burlington Resources Inc. (BR) today announced the promotion of Bobby 
S. Shackouls to the position of President and Chief Executive Officer of 
its oil and gas subsidiary, Meridian Oil Inc., in Houston, Texas. Mr. 
Shackouls, who had been Executive Vice President and Chief Operating 
Officer of Meridian, will report to Thomas H. O'Leary, Chairman, President 
and Chief Executive Officer of BR.

Mr. Shackouls, 43, replaces George E. Howison. Mr. Howison, who has 
held several financial positions with BR, supports the transfer of 
leadership to an executive with significant oil and gas operating 
experience. Mr. Howison will take early retirement and return to Seattle.

Mr. Shackouls has held various management positions in the oil and 
gas industry and prior to joining Meridian was President and Chief 
Operating Officer of Torch Energy Advisors Inc., the oil and gas affiliate 
of Torchmark Corporation. He holds a BS degree in Chemical Engineering 
from Mississippi State University.
