Item 8. Change in Fiscal Year

On May 19, 1994, the Board of Directors of Triton Energy Corporation

adopted a resolution providing that the date for the end of the fiscal year of

Triton Energy Corporation be changed from May 31 to December 31. A report

covering the seven-month transition period of June 1 through December 31, 1994

will be filed on Form 10-K.
