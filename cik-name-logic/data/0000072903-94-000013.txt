Item 5. Other Information.

Northern States Power Company and the International Brotherhood of Electrical
Workers (IBEW) have reached a tentative agreement for contracts to be
effective from January 1, 1994 through December 31, 1996. The package will
be presented to the IBEW members for a vote on May 2, 1994 with a full
recommendation of the Union's joint bargaining committee. In the meantime,
the Interim Agreement, entered into when the old Labor Agreement expired, has
been extended through May 31, 1994.
