Item 5. Other Events

On April 19, 1994, the Board of Directors of the Registrant
elected Gordon W. Kreh Chief Executive Officer. The 
promotion, which was announced at the Registrant's Annual
Meeting of Shareholders on April 19, 1994, follows his
election as President in September 1993. At the same time
it was announced that Wilson Wilde, who served as the
Registrant's President and Chief Executive Officer will
retire as an employee on May 1, 1994. He will remain
Chairman of the Executive Committee of the Board of
Directors.
