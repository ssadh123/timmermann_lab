JEFFERIES GROUP, INC. ANNOUNCES DEBT ISSUANCE

Los Angeles -- April 28, 1994 -- Jefferies Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: JEFG) announced
today the issuance of $50 million principal amount of 8.875% Senior Notes due
May 1, 2004. The net proceeds to the company are $49.3 million. The notes are
rated BBB- by Standard & Poor's Corporation and were placed privately by
Jefferies Group's principal subsidiary, Jefferies & Company, Inc.
