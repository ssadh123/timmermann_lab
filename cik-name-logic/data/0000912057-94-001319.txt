Item 5. Other Events.

On April 4, 1994, Best Buy Co., Inc., announced a 2-for-1 stock split,
payable in the form of a stock dividend. Shareholders of record on Thursday,
April 14, 1994, will receive one additional share for every share held, to be
issued on Thursday, April 28, 1994. After the split, the company will have
approximately 41.7 million shares outstanding.
