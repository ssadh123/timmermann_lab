ITEM 5. OTHER EVENTS.

On March 18, 1994, the board of directors of the Registrant filled two
vacancies on the board by appointing two new directors. Mr. David D. Mandarich,
Executive Vice President - Real Estate of the Registrant, was appointed as a
Class III director (term expires in 1994) and Herbert T. Buchwald was appointed
as a Class I director (term expires in 1995). Mr. Mandarich will be nominated
for re-election as a director at the 1994 Annual Meeting of Shareowners, and
Mr. Buchwald will be nominated for re-election as a director in 1995.
