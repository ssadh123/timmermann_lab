ITEM 5 - Other Events

On December 6, 1994, Philip T. Ashton announced to the Board
of Directors of Yankee Energy System, Inc. (the "Company") his
intention to retire as Chief Executive Officer effective March 1,
1995. Effective March 1, 1995, Branko Terzic, President and Chief
Operating Officer will assume the additional position of Chief
Executive Officer. Mr. Ashton will continue as Chairman of the
Board. Mr. Terzic was also elected a director of the Company on
December 6, 1994.
