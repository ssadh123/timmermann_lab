Item 5. Other Events.

On July 18, 1994, the Board of Governors of the Federal
Reserve System approved the proposed merger of BankAmerica
Corporation ("BAC") and Continental Bank Corporation
("Continental"), subject to compliance with certain conditions. 
BAC expects the merger to close within a few weeks after the
expiration of a mandatory 30-day waiting period, subject to
satisfaction of the remaining conditions set forth in the merger
agreement between BAC and Continental.
