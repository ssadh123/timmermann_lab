Item 5. OTHER EVENTS

On September 30, 1994, Ferrellgas, Inc., the General
Partner of Ferrellgas Partners, L.P. and Ferrellgas, L.P. (the
"Partnerships"), entered into a definitive Purchase Agreement with
Vision Energy Resources, Inc. ("Vision") for the purchase of the
propane business owned and operated by Vision for a cash purchase
price of $45 million. The closing of the transaction is subject to
customary conditions, including the expiration of the applicable
waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements
Act. Following the closing of the transaction, the General Partner
intends to transfer the assets of Vision to Ferrellgas, L.P.
