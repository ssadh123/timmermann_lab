Item 5. Other Events.

On August 10, 1994, National Property Investors, Inc. ("NPI"), 

the parent of NPI Equity Investments II, Inc. ("NPI Equity"), 

entered into an agreement with an affiliate ("Apollo") of Apollo 

Real Estate Advisors, L.P. to sell to Apollo up to one-third of 

the stock of NPI. The consummation of the transaction is 

subject to satisfaction of a number of conditions customary for

transactions of this type. Upon closing, Apollo would be 

entitled to representation on the board of directors of NPI 

Equity. NPI Equity controls the general partner of Registrant.
