Item 5. Other Events.

On September 23, 1994, Acme Metals Incorporated entered into an
agreement to sell 375,000 shares of common stock ("Shares") at a price of
$24.00 per share to Raytheon Engineers & Constructors, Inc. This sale
closed on October 7, 1994.

The Shares have not been registered under the Securities Act of 1993
as amended or under the laws of any state of the United States and may not
be offered or sold absent registration or an applicable exemption from
registration.
