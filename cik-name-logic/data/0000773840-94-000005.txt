Item 5. Other Events
------------

The Registrant issued today the following press release:

"AlliedSignal Inc. And BASF End Joint Venture Talks
---------------------------------------------------

AlliedSignal and BASF have ended their talks to create a 
joint venture of their textile and carpet fibers businesses.

The companies said that their respective businesses were
well positioned in their markets but they could not reach a final
agreement on a mutually satisfactory way to integrate them in a
joint venture."
