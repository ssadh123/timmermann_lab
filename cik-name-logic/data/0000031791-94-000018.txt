Item 5. Other Events

On August 3, 1994, the Company was informed by the U.S. Department of
Energy that the bid of Idaho Applied Technologies, a joint venture
comprised of EG&G, Inc., BNFL, Inc., and Fluor Daniel Environmental
Services, Inc., for the expanded contract at the Idaho National
Engineering Laboratory was unsuccessful.

Under the current contract at the Idaho National Engineering Laboratory,
which expires September 30, 1994, The Company reported $155 million in
net sales and $5 million of operating profit in the first six months of
1994.
